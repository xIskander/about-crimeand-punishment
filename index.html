Summary

Crime and Punishment by Fyodor Dostoevsky is a story of a young student named Rodion Raskolnikov who decides to murder someone and is forced to deal with the consequences that will soon come. This book is about redemption, manipulation, and the "mood swings" Raskolnikov's experiences through this book.
